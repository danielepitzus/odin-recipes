Spaghetti carbonara

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 100 g guanciale (or pancetta)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50 g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (for pasta water)

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Procedure:

  1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Cut guanciale into cubes and cook in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs with grated Pecorino and black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, keep a little cooking water.
  5. Mix pasta with guanciale (off heat).
  6. Add egg mixture, stirring quikly to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Adjust texture with pasta water. Serve immediately.

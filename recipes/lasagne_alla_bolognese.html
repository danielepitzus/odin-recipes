Lasagne alla Bolognese

Ingredients (4 servings):

  • 250 g lasagna sheets
  • 400 g ground beef
  • 200 g tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 500 ml béchamel sauce
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese
  • olive oil, salt, pepper

Cooking time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Procedure:

  1. Cook onion, carrot, celery in olive oil.
  2. Add meat, brown it.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer 30 minutes.
  4. Layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, Parmesan.
  5. Repeat layers.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes.

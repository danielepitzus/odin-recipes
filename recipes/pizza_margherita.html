Pizza Margherita

Ingredients (2 pizzas):

  • 500 g flour (00 type)
  • 325 ml water
  • 10 g salt
  • 3 g dry yeast
  • 300 g canned peeled tomatoes
  • 250 g mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil

Cooking time: 2-24h (dough fermentation) + 10 min baking

Difficulty: Medium

Procedure:

  1. Mix flour, water, yeast; then add salt.
  2. Knead until smooth, let rise 2-24 hours.
  3. Divide dought, stretch into circles.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, olive oil.
  5. Bake at highest oven temp (~250°C) for 7-10 minutes

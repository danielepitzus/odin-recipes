Kitchen Cacciatore

Ingredients(2-3 servings):

  • 600 g chicken pieces
  • 200 g tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 ml red wine
  • 50 g olives
  • Rosemary, salt, pepper
  • Olive oil

Cooking time: 50 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Procedure:

  1. Brown chicken in olive oil
  2. Add onion and garlic, sauté.
  3. Pour wine and let it evaporate.
  4. Add tomato sauce, olives, rosemary.
  5. Simmer covered for 40 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with bread polenta

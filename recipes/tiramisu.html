Tiramisu

Ingredients(4 servings):

  • 250 g mascarpone
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g sugar
  • 200 g ladyfingers
  • 200 ml espresso coffee
  • Cocoa powder

Coking time: 30 min + 4 hours chilling

Difficulty: Easy

Procedure:

  1. Separate eggs; whip yolks with sugar.
  2. Add mascarpone to yolks.
  3. Beat egg whites until stiff and fold in.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee quickly.
  5. Layer cream and biscuits.
  6. Dust with cocoa and chill at least 4 hours.
